Interactive Plotting Tool

Plot straight lines or curves by adding points, and see their formulas!

Click on the canvas to start plotting!

Instructions:

  1. Select "Straight Line" or "Curve" as your plot type.
  2. Click anywhere on the canvas to add points.
  3. For a **straight line**, you need at least two points.
  4. For a **curve**, add multiple points to define the shape. The tool will attempt to fit a polynomial to your points.
  5. The formula for the plotted line or curve will appear below.
